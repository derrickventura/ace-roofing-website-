ACE Roofing and Construction LLC — Business Website
Live site: aceroofnc.com
A fully responsive business website built for ACE Roofing and Construction LLC, a roofing company based in Salisbury, NC. Designed, developed, and deployed as a personal freelance project.

About the Project
ACE Roofing needed a professional online presence to showcase their services, projects, and contact information. This site was built from scratch with no frameworks or templates — just hand-written HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
The goal was a fast, clean, mobile-first site that felt trustworthy to potential customers and ranked well in local search results.

Features

Fixed navigation bar with mobile hamburger menu and smooth animation
Hero section with full-bleed photo background
Service cards with hover-reveal descriptions and mobile tap support
Project photo gallery with 17 images and show more / show less toggle
About section with company stats, vision, mission, and story
SEO optimized with meta tags, geo tags, and semantic HTML
SSL secured via Netlify
Fully responsive at 768px and 480px breakpoints


Tech Stack

HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript
Google Fonts (Oswald + DM Sans)
Netlify (hosting + automatic SSL)
Namecheap (domain registrar)
